# Merge Sort

Array = [16,21,11,8,12,22]  

**Solution**  
Step 1: [16 21 11] [8 12 22]  
Step 2: [16 21] [11] [8 12] [22]  
Step 3: [16] [21] [11] [8] [12] [22]  
Step 4: [16 21] [8 11] [12 22]  
Step 5: [8 11 16 21] [12 22]  
**Result** [8 11 12 16 21 22]   

**Big-O-Notation** O(nlogn) = O(6*log6) = 4,67
